Infotute Education Management System

Our Education Management Solution is a web based management system which enables an institute to use and operate many of integrated and inter-related modules to manages the administration efficiently. It is fully browser based application which includes online access to bring parents, teachers, and students on a common interactive platform. The solution simplifies and automates the administration process right from admissions, class management, examinations, library management, fee management, attendance, time table.

Infotute Online Classroom Application

Our Live Online Classroom Application is an unique solution for the educational institutes to conduct classes from a remote location. It enables the teacher and students to come to a common platform for teaching and learning , using real time technology. Graphical representations, pictures, online videos, notes, assignments, and worksheets can be shared and viewed by the students in real time. The solution has become very convenient and effective to access round the clock, with our high end training and support staff, especially in the pedagogy field.

Apexaspire SMS Management Solution

Our SMS Management Solution is a web based application promoting communication amongst the various stakeholders of an organisation. It is used for both transactional and promotional purpose. Any important information, notification and promotion can be communicated and executed using our SMS management solution, wherein the messages are transmitted real time to the end users. Elasticity to choose among different packages based on their validity ensures optimum usage and cost efficiency among the clients.

Infotute Automated attendance system

Our automated attendance management solution is based on RFID technology, where in the attendance is fetched in real time, and recorded in the application. We provide the necessary advisory, installation and support services for tracking system of attendance management and identity recognition, with widely acclaimed customer and technical support, to gauge and monitor the performance of employees or attendance of the students. The data fetched is accurate and precise, to suit the organisations need.
